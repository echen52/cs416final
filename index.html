This visualization shows the relationship between the 3 Pointers Made/Allowed on NBA Teams' Offensive/Defensive Efficiency. Conclusion

3-point shooting is an important component of NBA team performance. From these visualizations done on the 2024 NBA regular season, it appears that there is more impact for 3-point shooting on NBA team offense than there is for minimizing 3-point shooting on NBA team defense. A possible explanation is that NBA teams and coaches can better control team offense and shooting with changing lineups and shot selection, rather than controlling opponent shooting abilities and how it may affect their team defense. Further studies for defense could incorporate rim makes (layups and dunks), since studies have shown that defense is more correlated with these shots rather than 3-point shooting.
